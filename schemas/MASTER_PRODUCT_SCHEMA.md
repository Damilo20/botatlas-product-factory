## BotAtlas Rule

Every published product must satisfy the following requirements:

- Completeness Score ≥ 95%
- AI Confidence Score ≥ 90%
- Verification Status = Verified
- At least 3 trusted sources
- At least 10 images when available
- At least 1 video when available
- Technical specifications validated
- Marketplace information verified
- Affiliate opportunities evaluated
