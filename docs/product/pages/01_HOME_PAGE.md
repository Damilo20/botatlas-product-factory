# Purpose

The Home Page is the primary public entry point into BotAtlas.

It introduces the platform, establishes trust, communicates value, and provides immediate access to the Product Intelligence Engine.

The Home Page should answer three questions within five seconds.

1. What is BotAtlas?

2. Why should I trust BotAtlas?

3. What can I do here?

---

# Mission

Convert first-time visitors into active users by immediately demonstrating the value of evidence-backed product intelligence.

---

# Business Objectives

The Home Page exists to

- introduce BotAtlas
- build trust
- encourage product discovery
- encourage AI interaction
- encourage product comparison
- generate marketplace traffic
- grow user registrations

---

# Success Criteria

A first-time visitor should be able to

- understand BotAtlas in less than five seconds

- perform a search immediately

- ask an AI question

- open a product page

- compare products

without any onboarding.
