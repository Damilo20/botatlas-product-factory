# Executive Summary

BotAtlas is an AI-powered robotics intelligence, commerce, and operations platform.

Its purpose is to become the world's most trusted destination for discovering, comparing, buying, operating, servicing, and understanding robots.

Rather than acting as a simple online catalog, BotAtlas serves as the central operating system connecting every participant in the robotics ecosystem.

Manufacturers.

Dealers.

Businesses.

Consumers.

Developers.

Researchers.

Investors.

Educators.

Service Providers.

Every interaction begins with trusted data.

Every workflow is enhanced through artificial intelligence.

Every business transaction is supported through automation.
