# BotAtlas Launch Roadmap v1.0

> The execution roadmap for bringing BotAtlas from architecture to a public product.

Every phase should move BotAtlas closer to public launch.

---

# Guiding Principle

Every development cycle must satisfy two objectives:

1. Strengthen the long-term architecture.
2. Move BotAtlas closer to public availability.

If a task does neither, it should be reconsidered.

---

# Phase 1 — Foundation

Status: ✅ Complete

Completed

- Brand Bible
- System Architecture
- Capability Catalog
- Intelligence Lifecycle
- Core Domain Design
- Engineering Standards

Outcome

A stable foundation capable of supporting long-term growth.

---

# Phase 2 — Public MVP

Status: 🚧 In Progress

Objective

Publish the first publicly accessible version of BotAtlas.

Success Criteria

A visitor can search for a product and view a complete product page generated by the platform.

Core Deliverables

- Product research pipeline
- Product normalization
- Product enrichment
- Airtable repository
- Product page generation
- Search capability
- AI-generated summaries
- SEO metadata
- Affiliate integration
- Public website

---

# Phase 3 — Scale

Status: Planned

Objective

Expand from dozens of products to thousands.

Core Deliverables

- Automated ingestion
- Continuous research
- AI-assisted enrichment
- Monitoring
- Bulk publishing

---

# Phase 4 — Intelligence Platform

Status: Future

Objective

Transform BotAtlas into an autonomous product intelligence platform.

Core Deliverables

- Autonomous research agents
- Continuous knowledge updates
- Product comparison intelligence
- Market intelligence
- Commercial optimization

---

# Definition of Launch

BotAtlas is considered launched when:

- The public website is live.
- Users can search products.
- Product pages are generated automatically.
- Search engines can index the platform.
- Affiliate links are active.
- Revenue can be generated.
